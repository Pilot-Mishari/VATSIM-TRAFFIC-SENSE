9. ATC station availability may influence traffic density
Status: Hypothesis requiring historical validation
It is believed that ATC availability influences pilot traffic decisions. However, the level of influence varies depending on:
airport popularity,
region,
ATC position type,
time of day,
and historical activity patterns.

The system should prioritize observed historical correlations over hardcoded assumptions whenever sufficient data exists.
